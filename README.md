# CRM Sales Opportunities Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes CRM sales opportunity data to evaluate sales performance, identify revenue drivers, measure pipeline efficiency, and uncover actionable business insights. The analysis focuses on sales teams, products, customers, and regional performance to support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Problem

Sales organizations generate large volumes of opportunity data, but without proper analysis it is difficult to identify top-performing sales representatives, profitable products, high-value customers, and bottlenecks in the sales pipeline.

The objective of this project is to transform raw CRM data into meaningful insights that help improve revenue generation and sales effectiveness.

## Key Insights

* Identified top-performing sales agents and managers.
* Analyzed revenue contribution by products and product series.
* Evaluated customer sectors generating the highest revenue.
* Measured regional office performance.
* Analyzed pipeline stages to identify conversion bottlenecks.
* Measured average sales cycle duration and deal performance.

## Business Recommendations

* Focus sales efforts on high-performing products and sectors.
* Replicate successful strategies used by top-performing sales agents.
* Improve conversion rates at pipeline stages with the highest drop-off.
* Prioritize high-value customer segments and enterprise accounts.
* Optimize regional sales strategies based on performance metrics.
